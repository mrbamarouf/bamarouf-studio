# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Visitors arriving from the Bamarouf family network who need to understand the shared house, distinguish the three independent specialist practices, and choose the correct destination without navigating a combined-service agency site.

## Product Purpose

BAMAROUF STUDIO is the architectural entrance to three complete personal brands: TARIK BAMAROUF for digital experiences, NOOR BAMAROUF for graphic design, and KHALED BAMAROUF for systems engineering. Success means the visitor understands the philosophy of the house and confidently enters one specialist's existing website.

## Brand Personality

Monolithic, hushed, precise. The experience should convey purpose, heritage, confidence, and calm through the atmosphere of a private architectural residence or gallery.

## Anti-references

Generic agency, portfolio, SaaS, startup, or corporate templates. No bright technology palettes, glassmorphism, cyber glow, crowded service cards, developer illustrations, fake contact details, pricing, packages, statistics, or generic marketing language. No abstract CSS-only doors, empty black panels, or thin outlines standing in for real architecture.

## Design Principles

1. Architecture is navigation: movement through the house leads naturally to three doors.
2. Preserve independence: each specialist has a complete identity and destination of their own.
3. Let restraint carry confidence: short copy, deliberate spacing, and controlled motion.
4. Use materials and light as meaning, not decoration.
5. Give Arabic and English equal, intentionally composed experiences.
6. Use the approved three-door photograph only in the hero. Treat the mood board as an architectural universe, then give every other scene a unique environment, camera angle, material focus, and light composition within that universe.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use semantic structure, keyboard-accessible navigation, visible focus states, readable contrast, correct Arabic shaping and RTL composition, and a complete reduced-motion alternative. Desktop is the approved phase, while the architecture must remain safe for a future dedicated mobile phase.
